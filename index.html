About SIES

The South Indian Education Society (SIES) established in 1932 at Matunga, is a pioneer in the field of education, in this metropolis. The Society has been serving the cause of education and has carved for itself a niche, as a provider of quality and value based education from Nursery to Doctoral level in a wide variety of fields. The institute seeks to achieve the educational mission by focussing on the modes of inquiry, which strengthens thinking skills and provides extensive field experiences, to bring together theory and practices. "This society should sincerely serve the cause of education and the educational needs of the common man of this cosmopolitan city" -SIES Mission set by our founder Shri M. V . Venkateshwaran in 1932 Mission 2025
